About Us

About Us   __

Henan Ocean Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. is a well-known domestic petroleum extraction, refining, fractionation and biodiesel production enterprise with its operating headquarters in Zhengzhou, China. Our products are widely used in agriculture, people's livelihood products and many other industries, and help customers in these industries increase performance and improve efficiency. The company is committed to providing perfect after-sales service for every customer, adheres to customer-oriented, and improves the quality of the entire industry with its own high-quality products and services.
We have more than 50 senior engineers, majoring in technical process, mechanical, electrical, PLC control, etc. Long-term employment of senior professors from Henan University of Technology. Experts from relevant scientific research institutions and international petroleum experts. Strong R&D engineering design capabilities, and complete scientific research, design, office hardware facilities and international advanced computer design software. We have also established a joint laboratory with Henan University of Technology.
Committed to the development of new products and new technologies, and has more than 40 patents and proprietary technologies. Our production lines in 32 provinces in China (33 provinces in total) are running well. We provide many oil and fat companies with complete sets of oil preparation, refining, fractionation, winterization, dewaxing and degreasing, phospholipids, biodiesel, hydrogenation and other deep processing equipment and service.
Henan Ocean Intelligent Equipment Co., Ltd. has developed new continuous physical refining technology and equipment for vegetable oil, palm oil, cottonseed oil and animal oil separation technology and equipment, rice oil refining technology and equipment, tea seed oil processing technology and equipment, biological Diesel production process and equipment, etc.+
